Quantic Telecom :

Quantic Telecom is a french ISP created by students for students. The goal is to provide a high speed internet access to students at low prices. The structure was first an non-profit organization, now it is a limited liability company. We're deploying a radio electrical backbone over Rouen, in France.

In this adventure, I have learnt many things in many domains. I have improved my basic skills in network, working on bigger architectures, but also in communication and team work.
